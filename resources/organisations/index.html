Newspapers & Publications (for Leaving Cert Economics)

These sources are ideal for “current issues” and evaluation. Use them to (i) describe what’s happening, (ii) apply LC theory, and (iii) support your argument with real examples.

The Business Post

Strong Irish coverage of policy, business and finance. Great for LC essays on budgets, taxation, housing, inflation, investment, and the Irish banking system—often with clear, practical detail.

  • Home
  • About / what it covers
  • Economic Outlook Forum (example of their events/coverage)

Irish Independent

Daily business and economy reporting with lots of Irish examples. Useful for LC questions where you must propose policy responses (taxes/subsidies/regulation) and evaluate pros/cons.

  • Business hub
  • Irish business (Ireland-focused)
  • Money / personal finance (household economics)

The Irish Times

High-quality Irish coverage and analysis. Excellent for LC “evaluate” answers—often provides balanced arguments on budgets, housing, growth, inequality, and Ireland’s position in the EU/global economy.

  • Business hub
  • Economy section

Financial Times

Best for international macro: interest rates, inflation, exchange rates, globalisation, trade, and financial markets. Great when you want to compare Ireland/EU/US responses to economic shocks.

  • Economy
  • Markets
  • Markets data (indices, bonds, FX)

The Economist

Very strong for “big picture” explanation: why inflation happens, how central banks respond, global trade tensions, development issues, and policy trade-offs. Great for improving evaluation depth in long answers.

  • Finance & economics topic hub
  • Economic & financial indicators

RTÉ News

Excellent Ireland-specific reporting on inflation, budgets, wages, housing and the wider economy—often written in plain English, which helps LC students translate theory into real-world meaning.

  • RTÉ Business
  • RTÉ Business on X

How to customise (fast)

  • Logos: put images in /assets/logos/ and rename the src paths as needed.
  • Logo size: change width:72px;height:72px; (do both; e.g. 84px).
  • Roundness: change border-radius:14px; (999px = circle).
  • Space between logo + text: change gap:16px;.
  • Space between cards: change the margin-top: 16px; on each card.
