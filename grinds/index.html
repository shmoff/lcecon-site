Book Grinds

One-to-one and small-group Leaving Cert Economics grinds — focused, exam-driven, and built around real past-paper style.

Grinds service

What you get

  • structured weekly plan aligned to the Leaving Cert syllabus
  • topic-by-topic notes along with exam-style worksheets
  • diagram practice (with feedback and model answers)
  • timed questions and marking schemes to learn how to score top marks
  • help with study strategy, time management, and exam technique

How sessions work

  • Format: online (via Zoom/Teams) or in-person (if available)
  • Length: 60–90 minutes
  • Focus: weak areas first, then exposure to exam-style questions

If you send a short list of topics you’re struggling with, I’ll suggest a plan for the next 2–3 sessions.

Best for

  • students who need structure and consistent practice
  • students lost on how to tackle the large amounts of theory content in the course
  • students who want clearer diagrams & stronger evaluation answers
  • last-minute revision and exam preparation
